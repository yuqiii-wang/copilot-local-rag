FUT-402

Futures Expiration and Settlement Logic

TypeStory
StatusTo Do
PriorityHigh

Description

Futures contracts (e.g., ES-DEC24) have specific expiration dates.

The system currently treats them as perpetual until manually removed.


We need a job that runs EOD (End of Day) to identify expiring contracts.


Tasks

- Create `SettlementService`.

- Identify positions in contracts expiring Today.

- For Cash-settled futures, calculate PnL against the settlement price and close position.

- For Physical-settled, trigger a notification (auto-exercise is out of scope for now).


Related Module: `com.trading.dummy.model.Future`
